Empowering Student Voices

A secure, transparent, and modern approach to student council elections at Informatics College Northgate.

Our Mission

The Informatics Online Voting System was developed to modernize the traditional form-based election process. Our goal is to provide a platform that ensures absolute transparency, accessibility, and security for every student casting their ballot.

By digitizing the student council elections, we eliminate manual counting errors, reduce paper waste, and allow students from all strands (GAS, HUMSS, ABM, ICT) to participate effortlessly from any device.

System Core Values

Integrity & Security

Built with robust database security and One-Vote policies to ensure every election is fair and tamper-proof.

Transparency

Live, real-time analytics allow both administrators and students to monitor election progress with complete clarity.

Efficiency

From filing candidacy to casting a ballot, the entire process takes minutes, completely eliminating manual tallying.
